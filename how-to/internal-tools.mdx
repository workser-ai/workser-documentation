### Create Project in Product Page

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create new Project" defaultOpen={false} icon="window">
    1. Open Dashboard
    2. Navigate to `Products` tab in sidebar

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 21.55.35.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at21.55.35.png)
    3. Click `Create New Project` button and input your requirements

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 21.57.07.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at21.57.07.png)
    4. Click `Create Project` and wait AI Agent setup for a minute
    5. Redirect to Prebuilt UI page of your software product and vibe !

    ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 12.32.16.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at12.32.16.png)

    <Tip>
      You can vibe code anytime within your dashboard like other vibe coding platform
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Publish your oroject 

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Publish project" defaultOpen={false} icon="cloud-arrow-up">
    1. Open Dashboard
    2. Navigate to `Products` tab in sidebar
    3. Click `Projects` 

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 22.02.20.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at22.02.20.png)
    4. Select project you want to pubish
    5. Redirect to Prebuilt UI page of your software product

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 22.03.13.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at22.03.13.png)
    6. Click `Publish` button in top right of the page

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 22.03.39.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at22.03.39.png)
    7. Once publish succeeded, you will see url in the top bar of the page change to your custom domain that just published

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 22.06.31.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at22.06.31.png)
    8. Click custom domain url to redirect to your own web app !

       ![Screenshot 2568-10-01 at 22.04.33.png](/images/Screenshot2568-10-01at22.04.33.png)

    <Tip>
      You can vibe code anytime within your dashboard like other vibe coding platform
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
